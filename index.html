AVENGERS ENDGAME

Revenue: $2.797 billion worldwide

In 2018, twenty-three days after Thanos killed half of all life in the universe, Carol Danvers rescues Tony Stark and Nebula from deep space and they reunite with the remaining Avengers—Bruce Banner, Steve Rogers, Thor, Natasha Romanoff, and James Rhodes—and Rocket on Earth.

AVENGERS INFINITY WAR

Revenue: $2,048,359,754 worldwide

In the film, the Avengers and the Guardians of the Galaxy attempt to prevent Thanos from collecting the six all-powerful Infinity Stones as part of his quest to kill half of all life in the universe.

AVENGERS AGE ULTRON

Revenue: $1,395,316,979 worldwide

Tony Stark creates the Ultron Program to protect the world, but when the peacekeeping program becomes hostile, The Avengers go into action to try and defeat a virtually impossible enemy together. Earth's mightiest heroes must come together once again to protect the world from global extinction.

THE AVENGERS

Revenue: $1,518,812,988 worldwide

In the film, Nick Fury and the spy agency S.H.I.E.L.D. recruit Tony Stark, Steve Rogers, Bruce Banner, and Thor to form a team capable of stopping Thor's brother Loki from subjugating Earth.

CHARACTERS

CAPTAIN AMERICA (STEVE ROGERS)

Captain America is the alter ego of Steve Rogers, a frail young artist enhanced to the peak of human perfection by an experimental "super-soldier serum" after joining the military to aid the United States government's efforts in World War II.

POWER: " THE SHIELD "

IRON-MAN (TONY STARK)

Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. ... Later, Stark develops his suit, adding weapons and other technological devices he designed through his company, Stark Industries. He uses the suit and successive versions to protect the world as Iron Man.

POWER: " THE SUIT "

THANOS

Thanos is a mutant member of the race of superhumans known as the Titanian Eternals. ... Demonstrating enormous superhuman strength, speed, stamina, immortality and invulnerability among other qualities, Thanos can absorb and project vast quantities of cosmic energy, and is capable of telekinesis and telepathy.

POWER: " strength, speed, stamina, durability, and agility Superhuman physiology of Eternals "

SPIDERMAN (PETER PARKER)

Spider-Man is a fictional character in the Marvel Comics as well as main protagonist and the most well-known superhero in the Marvel Universe. He is a character created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in August 1962.

POWER: " strength resulting from the bite of a radioactive spider "

BLACK WIDOW (NATASHA ROMANOFF)

The first and best-known Black Widow is a Russian agent trained as a spy, martial artist, and sniper, and outfitted with an arsenal of high-tech weaponry, including a pair of wrist-mounted energy weapons dubbed her "Widow's Bite". She wears no costume during her first few appearances but simply evening wear and a veil.

POWER: " master in the covert arts of espionage, infiltration, and subterfuge "

DOCTOR STRANGE (DOCTOR STEPHEN STRANGE)

Strange was a skilled neurosurgeon before nerve damage impaired his hands. Doctor Strange is described as "the mightiest magician in the cosmos" and "more powerful by far than any of your fellow humanoids" by Eternity, the sentience of the Marvel Universe.

POWER: " Mastery of magic Utilizes mystical artifacts "

ANT MAN (SCOTT)

Ant-Man is a 2015 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics characters of the same name: Scott Lang and Hank Pym. ... In the film, Lang must help defend Pym's Ant-Man shrinking technology and plot a heist with worldwide ramifications.

POWER: " the ability to shrink to subatomic size while keeping full-size strength, control ants with his mind, and change the size of objects around him "

HULK (BRUCE BANNER)

In his comic book appearances, the character is both the Hulk, a green-skinned, hulking and muscular humanoid possessing a vast degree of physical strength

POWER: " As Bruce Banner: Genius intellect As Hulk: Invulnerability Superhuman strength, speed, stamina, and durability Anger empowerment Regeneration "

THOR

hammer-wielding god associated with lightning, thunder, storms, sacred groves and trees, strength, the protection of mankind and also hallowing and fertility

POWER: " God of Thunder "

HAWKEYE (CLINT BARTON)

Hawkeye (Clinton Francis Barton) is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.

POWER: " Golden Archer, Goliath, Ronin "
